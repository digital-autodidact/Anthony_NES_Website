My Art Projects

About Me As An Artist

"I like drawing people going about their daily activities"

How I Got Started

I started drawing later in my life.

Why I Draw

Art is a way for me to express myself and my creativity. I enjoy creating art using various mediums and tools.

I have created a variety of drawings, paintings, and photographs. I find inspiration in people, buildings, and everyday life. I hope you enjoy viewing my art projects!

Favorite Art Mediums

I like to use these mediums to create art:

  • Charcoal
  • Watercolor
  • Pen and Ink
  • Acrylic Paint
  • Photography

My Vision

"Art is play. All I really want to do is play."

I aspire to become a portraiture artist who sketches people in public!

I want to become a semi-professional artist who specializes in using traditional media to create "street portraits" of people.

I want to create art that inspires others and puts smiles on faces.

I want to create representative art that illustrates my short stories.

I want my art to be showcased on the Gibbs Street Gallery owned by Visarts. The best way to do that is to participate in the VisArts Gibbs Street Residency program.

I want to sell my art at local arts festivals like the Cherry Creek Arts Festival so I can meet like-minded people

My Inspiration Sources

"If I have seen further than others, it is by standing upon the shoulders of giants."

As an artist, I find inspiration in two sources:

(1) The work of other visual artists and storytellers

Here are some of the artists whose work I admire:

  • Leonardo Da Vinci's Sketches
  • Ken Sugimori's artwork from Pokemon Generations 1-2
  • The Woke Salaryman's webcomics
  • Ted Slampyak
  • Zac Crawford's Street Portraits
  • Norman Rockwell's paintings
  • Pawel Kuczynski's satirical illustrations
  • Gavin Aung Than's "Zen Pencils" webcomic
  • centuriichan
  • Robert DeJesus
  • Zoe Dufour's Sculptures
  • Christine Wang's sketches
  • Eric Kim's Cindy Project
  • Coelasquid's "Manly Men Doing Manly Things" webcomic

(2) My own life experiences

I like to draw what I observe when I travel to new locations.

I like to draw people. I am more concerned with making people smile, than making art that'll end up in a gallery. Why? Art, for me, is a means to meet people and create beautiful things with them.

I like to draw buildings. I am especially love to draw bridges, Victorian-style houses, Tudor Fairytale-style houses and stone-and-timber log cabins.
