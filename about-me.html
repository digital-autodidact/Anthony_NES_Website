Who Am I?

Background

I'm a Marine Corps veteran with an IT background. I developed my skills by working in various tech roles ranging from helpdesk technician to network administrator. During my enlistment, I led and trained small teams of junior technicians. I developed a unique blend of technological, investigative and leadership skills that I bring over to my civilian career.

What Makes Me Special?

What sets me apart is my insatiable curiosity and my unique talent stack! I bring an eclectic mix of skills and experiences to every organization I choose to work for.

I introduce myself as a "digital autodidact" because I'm driven to learn new skills in programming, writing and traditional art.

This unique talent stack means I'm positioned to bring a holistic approach to solving problems. Whether programming the frontend of a mobile app or painting a person's portrait, I'm always looking to challenge myself to grow as a human being.

I'm a Man With A Second Brain!

"What's a second brain?" Glad you asked!

It's a concept I learned from the productivity expert Tiago Forte. It's a system of tools and methods that help me organize my life and work. I use it to store my ideas, notes, and projects in a way that's easy to access and share.

My second brain system is a centralized repository of my best ideas, insights and thoughts, which exists outside of my head (ie the "first" brain)

Think of it like a personal wiki where I create FAQs on different subjects!

The system is designed to augment my natural memory, and more importantly, free up my first brain (the one between my ears) to focus entirely on thinking and learning effectively.

What Are My Interests?

I don't have hobbies, I have passions.

Things that fascinate me, but that will never show up in my resume:

Gamification

Gamification is the application of game design elements and principles in non-game contexts to enhance engagement, motivation, and participation.

Retro Video Games & Pixel Art

I love the simple gameplay mechanics and the aesthetics associated with video games that were released between 1980s-2000s.

Italian Renaissance Art

The Italian Renaissance was a cultural and intellectual movement that emerged in Italy during the 14th to 17th centuries, marking a revival of interest in classical art, literature, and learning from ancient Greece and Rome.

Urban Sketching

Urban sketching is the practice of drawing and capturing the surroundings of urban environments in a spontaneous and observational manner, often done on-site in public spaces.

Street Photography

Street photography is a genre of photography that focuses on capturing candid moments and scenes in public spaces, often highlighting the everyday life, culture, and emotions of people in urban environments.

Kintsugi Pottery

Kintsugi is a Japanese art form that involves repairing broken pottery with lacquer mixed with powdered gold, silver, or platinum, highlighting and celebrating the object's history rather than hiding its flaws.

Music Box Melodies

I love the soft, delicate tunes produced by music boxes. It's something that soothes me.

The Dictionary of Obscure Sorrows

The Dictionary of Obscure Sorrowsis a creative project by John Koenig that aims to invent new words for emotions and experiences that are often difficult to articulate.
